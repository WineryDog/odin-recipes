Pad Kra Prao

Description

Pad Ka Prao, a very simple dish, minced meat with basil and chili

The ingredients for this recipe are easily available. Thai basil has purple stem, small leaves, and a slightly spicy taste of our basil, with anise and licorice aromas. The meat can also be pork or chicken, always checking that the cooking is complete.

Ingredients

  • 500 g ground veal (or beef) of first choice
  • 2 tablespoons peanut oil
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 3 red peppers, finely cut
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (can be found in oriental grocery stores)
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 handful of fresh basil
  • 1 fresh lime, cut in quarters

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a wok and add the garlic.
  2. Add the chili pepper and stir for about 2 minutes.
  3. Add the minced meat and break it with a wooden spoon.
  4. Bring the heat to the maximum and add the soy sauce, the fish sauce and the sugar.3 red peppers, finely cut
  5. Stir for 2 minutes (or until the meat is cooked).
  6. Turn off the fire and garnish with fresh basil and a few drops of lime juice.
  7. Serve immediately with Jasmine white rice.
