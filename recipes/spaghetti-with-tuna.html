Spaghetti with tuna

Description

A first dish easy to make, ideal for a spaghetti of the last minute and a perfect dinner-saving.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti 320 of the 1 g
  • Peeded tomatoes 400 of the g
  • Salt
  • Fresh basil
  • Tuna in oil (discomtted) 150 g
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Black pepper
  • 1 golden onion

Steps

  1. Drain the tuna fillet from the storage oil
  2. Clean the onion, slict it
  3. Heat the olive oil in a pan and add the sliced onion
  4. Let it wither over low heat for a few minutes stirring often; fray tuna with your hands
  5. Add it in a pan when the onion will be softened
  6. Let it brown for a couple of minutes always stirring. Now, crush the peeled with a 5 fork
  7. Pour them into the pan with tuna
  8. Let the sauce cook for about 10 minutes
  9. Meanwhile, boil the spaghetti and cook them "al dente"
  10. In the time of cooking the pasta, the sauce will also be ready
  11. Drain the spaghetti directly in the pan with tuna
  12. Season with ground pepper
  13. turn off the heat and perfume with the fresh basil leaves
  14. Stir and serve your hot tuna spaghetti!
